Learn how to use Shopping List App effectively

1. Create Your First List

Start by creating a shopping list. Click on "Create New List" from your dashboard or the lists page.

  • Give your list a descriptive name
  • Add a description to remember what you're shopping for
  • Choose a priority level (Urgent, High, Medium, Low)
2. Add Items to Your List

Once you have a list, start adding items you need to buy.

  • Specify the item name and quantity
  • Choose a category to organize your items
  • Set a priority level for each item
  • Add estimated price and notes if needed
3. Organize and Shop

Use categories and priorities to organize your shopping trip efficiently.

  • Group items by category (e.g., Dairy, Produce, Meat)
  • Focus on high-priority items first
  • Mark items as purchased when you buy them

Estimate Prices

Add estimated prices to get a rough idea of your total spending before shopping.

Mark as Purchased

Check off items as you buy them to keep track of your progress.
